---
title: Create a Link Collection
description: Organize multiple links into a collection.
---

## Overview

Collections help you group your links for easy access.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Collections page">
    Select "Collections" in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click New Collection">
    Give your collection a name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add links">
    Select links to include or create new ones.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save" to finish. Your collection is ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>
