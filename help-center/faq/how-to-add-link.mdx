## Overview

Adding a link in LinkSpot is quick and easy.

## Steps

1. In your LinkSpot dashboard, click "New Link".
2. Enter the URL and a title.
3. (Optional) Add a description or tags.
4. Click "Save" to add the link.

Your link will now appear in your project list. You can edit or organize it any time.
