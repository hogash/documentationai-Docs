---
title: How do I create a pin?
description: Steps to place a clickable pin on a product image in LinkSpot.
---

## Overview

Pins are clickable hotspots you place on product gallery images. Each pin links to another product, letting customers discover related items directly from the image.

## Prerequisites

Before creating a pin, make sure you have:
- The LinkSpot app embed block enabled in your theme
- A compatible theme (run the compatibility test if you haven't already)
- At least two products in your catalog (one to place the pin on, one to link to)

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Pins">
    In the LinkSpot sidebar, click **Pins**. You'll see a list of products in your store.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Product">
    Choose the product whose image you want to add a pin to. The product's gallery images will load.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Place the Pin">
    Click on the product image at the position where you want the pin to appear. A pin marker will be placed at that location.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Link to a Product">
    Select the product you want the pin to link to. When a customer clicks the pin on your storefront, they'll see information about this linked product.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a Style">
    Pick a pin style that matches your store's design. Options include Classic, Disk, Plus, Neon, and more. On Growth and Scale plans, all 12 styles are available.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to publish the pin. It will appear on the product image on your storefront.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
You can place multiple pins on a single product image. The maximum number of pins per image depends on your plan.
</Callout>
