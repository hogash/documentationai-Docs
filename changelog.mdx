## LinkSpot Launch

LinkSpot is now available on the Shopify App Store. Turn your product images into interactive, shoppable experiences.

### Core Features

- **Pins** -- Place clickable hotspots on product images that link to other products. 12 pin styles available including Classic, Disk, Plus, Neon, and custom SVG.
- **Product Sets** -- Display curated product groups on product images with three layout options: Button & Popup, Card Carousel, and Links List.
- **Annotations** -- Add text overlays to product images to highlight features, materials, or details.
- **Captions** -- Add caption overlays with configurable size and position.

### Platform

- Seamless Shopify theme integration via app embed block
- Automatic theme compatibility detection
- Four plans: Free, Starter, Growth, and Scale
- Product Quick View popup for in-page product browsing
- Manual CSS selector support for custom themes
- Multi-language support (Growth and Scale plans)
- Customizable color schemes, corner styles, and display settings
